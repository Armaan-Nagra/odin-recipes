Pizza

Description

The photo from above shows wood fired pizza margherita, you make the dough, let it rest, stretch it out, add ingredients on the pizza and then slide it into a wood fired oven at 500°C

ingredients

Note: this recipe makes 8 pizzas

  • 2.2 pound bag Caputo "00" flour
  • 5 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of fresh yeast
  • 2¼ cups of water
  • olive oil
  • ounce of tomato sauce (preferably mutti)
  • ounce of fresh mozzarella sliced
  • ounce of grade Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • ounce of grated pecorino cheese
  • 1/8 cup basil leaves

Steps

  1. Melt yeast into water in mixing bowl, add flour and begin mixing at the lowest speed for 15 minutes. Slowly add in the salt.
  2. Put on a counter and wrap in plastic to rest for 1 hour.
  3. Cut into dough balls (22 ounces each) and place in a tray. Put balls in a cooler to rest for use the next day, 24 hours recommended.
  4. Stretch dough into a circle and place on a pizza peel.
  5. Spread sauce on the dough and place fresh mozzarella on top.
  6. Place dough into wood-fired oven (or a counter-top pizza oven). Cook for 90-120 seconds spinning throughout until golden and delicious on all sides.
  7. Slice and then top with basil, Sicilian olive oil, Parmigiano-Reggiano and pecorino and serve!
